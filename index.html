PEACE CORPS RECIPES
Homemade Tahini
Requires:
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Vegetable Oil
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spread sesame seeds on baking sheet and bake for 8 to 10 minutes, but do not brown. Put sesame seeds in a blender or grind with a mortar and pestle, then add vegetable oil to make a smooth paste.
